SVG Circles

Circles have slightly different properties than boxes. The cy and cy properties are from the center of the circle, instead of the upper left corner. We use the 'circle' element to draw these.

Attributes include:

  • r - radius
  • cx/cy - Coordinates for center of circle
  • style:
    • fill - fill color
    • stroke - border stroke color
    • stroke-width - stroke width
    • opacity - amount of transparency. Between 0 and 1.
